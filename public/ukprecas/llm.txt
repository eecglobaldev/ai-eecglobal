## Definitions

UK Pre-CAS Interview: Mandatory 15-30 minute video credibility assessment by UK universities before CAS issuance. Evaluates genuine student intention, course knowledge, financial capability.

CAS: Confirmation of Acceptance for Studies. 14-character reference from UK university. Required for Student Route visa. Valid 6 months.

IHS: Immigration Health Surcharge. £1,035/year NHS access fee for students (2026). Increased from £470 in 2024.

Graduate Route: 2-year UK post-study work visa. No job offer required. 3 years for PhD.

Maintenance Funds: Financial requirement. London £12,006. Outside London £9,207. Plus tuition. 28-day holding rule.
